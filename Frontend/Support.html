Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a bicycle?
Visit our website, select the bicycle you want to rent, and complete the booking process online. You can also rent a bicycle by visiting one of our rental locations in person.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept credit cards and debit cards and all UPIs. Payment must be made online at the time of booking.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your rental start time for a full refund. If you cancel within 24 hours of your rental start time, no refund will be given.
